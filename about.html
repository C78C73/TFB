About Task Force Black

Elite Milsim Community - 100+ Strong

Our Mission

Task Force Black is a premier milsim community dedicated to tactical excellence and authentic military simulation. We bring together over 100 dedicated members from around the world who share a passion for realistic combat operations, teamwork, and tactical gameplay across Arma and Battlefield platforms.

Who We Are

Our community is primarily composed of active and prior service members of the United States Armed Forces, alongside committed members from countries across the globe. This unique blend of real-world military experience and dedicated simulation enthusiasts creates an environment of authenticity, professionalism, and camaraderie that sets Task Force Black apart.
