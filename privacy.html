Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

Introduction

Task Force Black ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website or interact with our gaming community.

Information We Collect

Our website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal information through this website.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

  • GitHub Pages: Hosts our website and may collect basic analytics (IP address, browser type, etc.)
  • Google Forms: If you submit recruitment or content forms, Google's privacy policy applies
  • CustomInk: Our merchandise store - their privacy policy applies to purchases
  • Discord: Community communications - Discord's privacy policy applies

Cookies

Our website uses local storage to remember your dark/light mode preference. This is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

External Links

Our website contains links to external sites (merchandise store, social media, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Community Participation

When you join our Discord server or participate in operations, you interact with platforms outside our website. Please refer to:

  • Discord's Privacy Policy for community communications
  • Game platform privacy policies (Steam, EA, Xbox Live, PlayStation Network)

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents and guardians should supervise online activities. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
